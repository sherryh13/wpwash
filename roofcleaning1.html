HIGH PRESSURE IS NEVER TO BE USED ON THE ROOF!

This is just good common sense,High pressure will remove the grit from the roofing and may start leaks.
 
WHATS ON MY ROOF?

Moss-Moss (disambiguation).
Moss grows roots into the roof shingle, high pressure or brushing will only remove the top layer. Roof treatments containing a Herbicide are appalled to your roof to kill the moss down to the root.

Black mold
Black mold is just that, its black,its ugly,and its growing on your roof and needs to be removed! Not only for the curb appeal but for numerous health reasons.

Lichens http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lichen
Lichens occur in some the most extreme environments on Earth. Lichens or fungus if you will, grow much the same as moss with roots into the shingle and are treated with the same process as moss .

Roof treatments
Moss & lichen Roof treatments are of a copper sulfate base
Black mold Roof treatments are of a sodium hydrochloride base
