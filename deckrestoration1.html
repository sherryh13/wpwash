Wayne power washing is a locally owned and operated company specializing in the restoration and refinishing of exterior wood. We are not a typical "Power Washing" company.  Wayne power washing enhances the appearance and protects exterior wood.

Wood is different. Yes, we own and often use pressure washers. But just as a sauce pan serves a master chef, a pressure washer is merely one tool of many used to efficiently prepare wood to accept a stain. And a dangerous tool at that.

Water pressure in untrained hands can cause severe damage to wood and personal injury. What works on concrete or vinyl siding is certainly not appropriate for wood. In fact, for our customers wishing to maintain their restored wood themselves, we strongly discourage the use of a high  pressure washer. It is not needed.

Wayne power washing is a professional wood restoration company. A combination of years of practical experience, product evaluation, and knowledge of wood matched with craftsmanship quality work assures that your exterior wood will not only look better than new, but will be protected from further damage.

You may be thinking "This is a service I need, but sounds expensive. All I want is a good looking deck". Two points in response. You cannot afford to leave your exterior wood unprotected. Not only does your property look less than its best, but to replace your exterior wood due to time and neglect is extremely expensive.

Our services are not cheap, but also not expensive. How can this be? Consider this. Cheap is unskilled labor, using inferior products and methods. Cheap is damage to your wood, or house, or surrounding landscape. Cheap is stain that does not last, or worse yet, stain that must be stripped in a year or two.
